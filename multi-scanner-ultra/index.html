Turn your phone into a powerful document scanner

Multi Scanner Ultra is a PDF scanner and document scanner for Android that captures crisp pages and arranges them into clean, multi-page PDFs — with offline OCR to make the text searchable and copyable. Snap receipts, contracts, whiteboards, or notes, organize them into folders with tags, and export a polished PDF whenever you need it.

Unlike cloud scanner apps that upload every page you scan, Multi Scanner Ultra is local-first: capture, OCR, editing, and PDF export all happen on your device. Your documents stay yours. It also doubles as a barcode and QR scanner, so one app covers document capture and code scanning.

Key features

  • Scan to PDF — capture multiple pages into a single, clean PDF.
  • Offline OCR — extract searchable, copyable text from your scans, on-device.
  • Barcode & QR scanning — read codes without a second app.
  • Organize — folders, tags, favorites, merge, duplicate, and reorder pages.
  • Edit pages — rotate, crop, move, copy, and delete individual pages.
  • Local export — save PDFs and image sets to your device, your choice.

What you can scan

Receipts Contracts ID cards Whiteboards Notes Invoices QR codes Barcodes

A privacy-first Cam Scanner alternative

Many scanner apps require an account and sync your scans to their servers. Multi Scanner Ultra needs no account and performs OCR and PDF generation locally, making it a strong Cam Scanner alternative for anyone who'd rather their documents didn't leave their phone. Cloud sync, collaboration, and ads are intentionally excluded.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free PDF scanner for Android?

A great free PDF scanner captures sharp pages, cleans up the background, arranges them into a multi-page PDF, and works without uploading your documents. Multi Scanner Ultra does all of this locally and adds offline OCR and barcode scanning at no cost.

Can I scan a document to PDF offline?

Yes. Multi Scanner Ultra is local-first, so you can capture pages, run OCR to extract text, and export a multi-page PDF without an internet connection. Nothing leaves your device.

Does the scanner have OCR text recognition?

Yes. Multi Scanner Ultra includes offline OCR that recognizes text in your scans so you can copy and search it later. OCR runs on-device, so your document content is not sent to any server.

Can it scan barcodes and QR codes?

Yes. In addition to document scanning, Multi Scanner Ultra reads barcodes and QR codes, so one app covers document capture and code scanning.

Is my scanned document uploaded to the cloud?

No. Multi Scanner Ultra is local-first. Scans, OCR text, tags, and PDFs are stored on your device unless you explicitly export them yourself. There is no forced cloud sync or account.
